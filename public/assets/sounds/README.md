# Sound Assets

## Required Files

Place your audio files in this directory:

### Background Music
- **background.mp3** - Haunting background music (looping)
- **background.ogg** - Alternative format for browser compatibility

### Recommendations
- Keep volume moderate (app sets to 30%)
- Use atmospheric, ambient horror music
- Avoid sudden loud sounds
- Loop-friendly (smooth transitions)
- Duration: 2-5 minutes recommended

### Suggested Sources
- Free horror music from:
  - FreePD.com
  - Incompetech.com (Kevin MacLeod)
  - YouTube Audio Library
  - Pixabay Music
  - Free Music Archive

### Example Search Terms
- "dark ambient music"
- "horror background music"
- "creepy atmospheric music"
- "haunted house music"
- "supernatural ambience"
